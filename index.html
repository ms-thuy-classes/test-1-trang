D. increasingly

✍️ Phần Fill-in-the-blanks (Điền từ)

Advertisements are displayed everywhere, making them an unavoidable part of our ______________. | everyday life
Companies launch major ______________ right before the Lunar New Year. | promotional campaigns
Advertisements ______________ appear during prime-time TV. | frequently

🔀 Phần Sắp xếp câu (Word Order)

Cấu trúc: Các từ xáo trộn (phân cách bằng /) | Câu đúng 
common / increasingly / life / in / daily / Advertisements / becoming / are / . | Advertisements are becoming increasingly common in daily life.
crucial / products / Advertising / promoting / is / for / . | Advertising is crucial for promoting products.

🔄 Phần Viết lại câu (Paraphrasing)

Cấu trúc: Câu gốc (gợi ý) | Câu trả lời đúng 
Advertisements are becoming more and more common in everyday life. (increasingly / daily) | Advertisements are becoming increasingly common in daily life.
Advertising is important for promoting products. (crucial / goods and services) | Advertising is crucial for promoting goods and services.
